Grilled Cheese

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet satisfying classic. Itâ€™s perfect for a quick lunch, snack, or even a light dinner. The combination of crispy, buttery bread with melted cheese inside makes for a comforting and delicious treat. You can make it in under 10 minutes with just a few basic ingredients: bread, cheese, and butter. This sandwich can also be customized by adding extras like tomatoes, ham, or bacon for a more gourmet experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Butter(softened, for easy spreading)

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread generously. This will give the bread a golden, crispy exterior when grilled.
  2. Place two slices of cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat
  4. Place the sandwich in the skillet, buttered side down. Cook for about 2-3 minutes or until the bread turns golden brown.
  5. Flip the sandwich carefully using a spatula, and cook the other side for another 2-3 minutes, until both sides are crispy and the cheese is fully melted.
  6. Remove from the pan, slice in half, and enjoy while the cheese is still gooey!
